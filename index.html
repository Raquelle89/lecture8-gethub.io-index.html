Langston Hughes

Langston Hughes was one of the most well-known American poets. Born on February 1, 1902, Some of his major influences include Carl Sandburg, Walt Whitman, and Paul Lawrence Dunbar.

Montage of a Dream Deferred by Langston Hughes

What happens to a dream deferred? Does it dry up like a raisin in the sun? Or fester like a sore— And then run

Let America Be America Again

Let America be America again.
Let it be the dream it used to be. Let it be the pioneer on the plain Seeking a home where he himself is free. (America never was America to me.) Let America be the dream the dreamers dreamed— Let it be that great strong land of love Where never kings connive nor tyrants scheme That any man be crushed by one above. (It never was America to me.)
